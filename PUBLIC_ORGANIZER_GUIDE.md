## How to Create an Event

- **Open Organizer Dashboard:** Sign in as an organizer and navigate to the Organizer Dashboard.
- **Start New Event:** Click the `Create Event` (or `New Event`) button.
- **Basic Details:** Fill in **Event Title**, **Description**, **Start/End Date & Time**, and **Category**.
- **Venue & Theater:** Select the venue and theater. If the platform supports a theater builder, open the `Theater Builder` to configure seating layout and sections.
- **Seating & Capacity:** Configure seat map or general admission capacity; set seat categories (e.g., VIP, General).
- **Ticket Types & Pricing:** Add one or more ticket types (name, price, quantity, per-ticket fees). Set availability windows and limits per order.
- **Service Charges & Taxes:** Add any service charge entries or taxes applied to ticket purchases.
- **Coupons & Discounts (optional):** Attach or create coupon codes to be used by buyers.
- **Uploads:** Add featured image(s) and any attachments (seat map images, PDF flyers).
- **Preview & Publish:** Preview the event listing, then `Publish` or set it to `Draft` if you want to finish later.

Tips:
- Keep the event description clear and include parking, accessibility, and age limits.
- Use the preview to verify seating layout and pricing before publishing.

## How to Check Orders for an Event

- **Go to Orders / Sales:** From the Organizer Dashboard, open the `Orders` or `Sales` section.
- **Filter by Event:** Use filters to select the specific event, date range, or ticket type.
- **Order List:** The list shows order ID, purchaser name/email, tickets purchased, gross amount, fees, and status.
- **View Order Details:** Click an order to see purchaser contact info, payment method, individual ticket codes/QRs, and refund status.
- **Manage Refunds/Exchanges:** If supported, use the order detail screen to issue full or partial refunds or exchange tickets according to your policy.
- **Export Orders:** Export filtered orders to CSV for reconciliation or accounting.

Common actions:
- Search by order ID or purchaser email for quick lookups.
- Re-send tickets or confirmation emails from the order detail page.

## How to View Event Analysis

- **Open Analytics/Reports:** From the Organizer Dashboard, open `Analytics`, `Reports`, or `Event Insights`.
- **Select Event & Timeframe:** Choose the event and timeframe you want to analyze.
- **Key Metrics:** Look for these standard metrics:
  - **Tickets Sold:** Number and percentage of capacity sold.
  - **Gross Revenue:** Total sales before fees and taxes.
  - **Net Revenue:** Earnings after fees, taxes, and refunds.
  - **Average Order Value (AOV):** Revenue divided by number of orders.
  - **Sales Over Time:** A time-series chart showing when sales occurred.
  - **Ticket Type Breakdown:** Sales by ticket category (VIP, General, etc.).
  - **Top Promo/Coupon Performance:** Which coupons drove the most sales.
- **Attendee Demographics (if available):** Age ranges, locations, or segments.
- **Scan/Check-In Data:** If scanning is enabled, view real-time check-ins and no-show rates.
- **Export & Share:** Export charts or CSV reports for stakeholders.

How to use analysis:
- Monitor sales velocity during promotional windows and adjust pricing or marketing.
- Use time-of-day sales trends to plan targeted promotions.
